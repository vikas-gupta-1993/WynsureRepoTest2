Wynsure contains the following entry points.

 

My Work

Tracks each individual’s tasks and projects.

 

§         To Do List: folder containing tasks assigned to the current user.

§         Completed Tasks: once finished all tasks are catalogued in this folder for future reference

§         Create a Task: manually create a new task for another user.

 

Products

Contains all existing insurance products. This is view only.

 

§         Individual Products: a catalogue of insurance packages to be sold to single persons or families.

§         Group Products: a catalogue of insurance packages designed for sale to organizations.

 

Customer service

Track customer requests and inquires.

 

§         Search for a Person: search by name, SSN, birthdates, or client ID. You may also sort results by choosing customer or broker.

§         Search for a Legal entity: Search by legal name, short name, client ID, the role the organization plays such as customer, broker, service provider, insurer, or choose a classification and it’s values such as legal structure: corporate or size: large.

§         File Customer Requests: document an incoming email, letter, or call.

§         Letters: generate commonly used letters from the templates provided.

§         Reports: generate commonly used reports from the templates provided.

§         Workflow Management: track all work for the department.

§  Team To Do List: contains new tasks for the team.

§  Tasks in Progress: shows the team tasks that are currently in process.

§  Completed Tasks: a complete inventory of the team’s finished tasks

§  Create a Task: manually create a new task for another user.

Product Factory

Creation of new insurance products.

 

§         Individual Products: search, create or modify insurance coverages to be sold to single persons or families.

§         Legal Entity Products: search, create or modify insurance coverages designed for sale to organizations.

§         Group Benefit Plan: search, create or modify an insurance package of coverages designed for sale to Legal Entities.

§         Legal Language: create, modify, or add existing policy provisions to a product.

§         External Tables and Formulas: define rules, tables, and indexes for use in benefit amount and rate calculations.

§  Rule: import, export, create, or modify formulas to be used to calculate benefit amounts, premiums, or eligibility.

§  Table: import, export, create, or modify tables for use in calculations.

§  Indexes: create and manage simple lookup tables that track incremental changes by date.

§         Customer Classification: attributes to be associated with policy subscribers. These fields will become available for searching and for product benefit calculations.

§  Subscriber Classifications: create and manage lists of factors which could affect rates and benefit amounts.

§  Persons Classifications: create and manage global categories for all persons.
