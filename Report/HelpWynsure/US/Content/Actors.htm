Definition

The Legal Entity represents an entity that has legal recognition by the government, such as a company.


A legal entity is defined by:

·                         Identification data (Legal Name, Legal address, )

·                         Other correspondences:  postal only, email or fax or telephone

·                         Involvements: it can be a customer, Broker, Issuing Company, Insurer, TPA, affiliate (for every involvement some data is defined)

·                         Bank accounts

·                         Relations: its relation with other legal entities or persons.

·                         Members: employees in the legal entity

·                         Contacts (a contact is defined by a label and an address chosen between legal entity addresses)

·                         Hierarchy: where the parent legal entity can be defined

Usage

A legal entity will be systematically used when the description of a company is necessary in Wynsure:

·                                 The companies that use Wynsure

·                                 The companies that sell a products (Issuing company)

·                                 The Company that receive some commission when a contract is signed (Broker or Producer)

·                                 The companies that is the Third Administrator of an other company (TPA)

·                                 The companies that buy insurance (Customer who signs an insurance contract)

·                                 The entity that provides certain service through the issuing company product (Service Provider or Health Care Provider)

·                                 Company that assumes the risk of insurance contract (Insurer).

·                                 Company that shares the risk of some insurance contract (Re-insurer)

·                                 Company whose member can be enrolled on a group benefit plan subscribed by an other company (Affiliate)

Folder Main

The main folder contains the identification data such as Tax Id, SIC code, Legal address, Situs State, Worksite States, and other the classification values versioned by date (Such as legal entity size and legal structure).

It is possible from Wynsure to define several Legal Entity Classifications (From the Administration Entry Point). Then the classifications automatically appear in the Main folder of every Legal Entity. Then you can define for each classification what the corresponding value is for the company.

 

Folder Other correspondences

 

This folder contains the other contact information such as postal address, telephone number and Email address. Each type of correspondence is presented as following,

 

o        Postal address, by which letters can reach the actor.

o        Phone number, by which phone calls can reach the actor.

o        eMail address, by which eMails can reach the actor.

 

Folder Involvements

 

This folder presents the different roles that a Legal entity can play in Wynsure: Customer, Broker, Issuing Company, TPA (Third party administrator), Insurer, etc.

Sub Folder Customer

§         Sub Folder Issuing Companies

 

The Client id identifies the customer.  A customer Legal Entity must be declared its Issuing Company (Companies) to be able to subscribe a group benefit plan with this Issuing Company.

 

§         Sub Folder Management

 

Web Login defines the login name and password of the customer when using web service. In management setting,  you can define 1) the workflow of the client, thats to say the person or legal entity the customer has been through in certain function domain, and 2) the authorization unit, which precise his/her attached organization unit.  (Every Legal Entity can be attached to an Organization Unit. An organization unit is one element of the description of the organization of the company managing the information system. Its window is shown below)

Internal contacts contains information of the customers contacts in certain period.

Sub Folder Issuing Company

 

The Issuing Company is the company that sells the insurance products and provides the insurance contract. Resulting contracts are signed by the customer and the Issuing Company.

 

When a product is defined in Wynsure, this product must be attached to an Issuing Company. Wynsure is an application multi-Issuing Company. In the same Information System, several Issuing Companies can be defined. Every product, contract and entities around will be attached to one Issuing Company. This allows a company to manage its Products/Contracts separately depending on its geographical structure for instance. It can also allow a company to manage Products/Contracts of another company that has no Information System.

 

§         Sub Folder Main

 

In the Main folder, you can define The format of generated Invoice numbers/Contract number/Compensation and Statements Ids for this issuing company

 

§         Sub Folder Settings

This folder defines the currencies managed by this issuing company and its possible payment channels.

 

§         Sub Folder Accounts

Wynsure generates the Global suspense accounts for issuing company as a default account.

 

Sub Folder Third Party Administrator

A TPA is an independent company hired by a group to administer its self-insured plans. The TPA adjudicates claims, compares them to the benefit plan and makes the decision to write checks from the companies funds.

 

You define which company is the TPA for a period (From Begin date to End date)

 

Sub Folder Affiliate

An affiliate is a company whose members can be enrolled in the Group Benefit Plan of another Company. For instance, if AOO is the affiliate of Nike, then the Members of AOO can subscribe to the GBP defined for Nike.

 

A Legal entity can be the affiliate of several companies for a defined period (From Begin Date to End Date).

 

Sub Folder Broker

A broker is a a State-licensed Person or Legal Entity who negotiates Policies of insurance with Insurers on behalf of the Insured, receiving a commission from the Insurer for placement and other services rendered.

 

The Broker is the subscriber of Compensation Contracts.
A broker is a complement to an existing Legal entity (or existing person) so as to define additional data such as:

·                                            Broker Id

·                                            Licenses agreements

·                                            Web Log in account as broker

·                                            Etc.

Sub Folder Service provider

Service provider is a legal entity that sells non-insurance service through the Issuing Company product. You can give additional descriptions to it.

 

Sub Folder Health care provider

 

Health care provider is a doctor, hospital, nurse, etc. that can be used in claims.

A Provider Id can be defined to identify the health care provider.

 

§         Sub Folder Main

You can give additional information about the health care provider such as his type and his specialties (Optical Dispensary, Surgery-oral, etc)

 

§         Sub Folder Specification

The folder where contains the detail information about the health care provider, including Generation suffix, Credential code, UPIN number, License State, Business zip code, filler and Administrator. (Administrator is not available when the involvement concerns a Person instead of a Legal Entity)

 

§         Sub Folder Description

You can give description of the health care provider in this folder.

 

§         Sub Folder Structure (This folder is not available when the involvement concerns a Person instead of a Legal Entity)

If the health service provider is not independent, which is represented by selecting the check box Has a structure, you can describe its structure.

 

A dialogue window will appear as below by double clicking on the tree root, where you can define detail data of the health care provider unit.

 

Sub Folder Insurer

An insurer is a company that takes the risk associated to an insurance product.

You will have to define in a coverage, which company takes the risk, then you are given the list of Insurers.

 

An insured Id is attached to an Insurer.

You can consult or modify the existing accounts as shown below ( Three types of account are considered in Wynsure, US Bank Account, IBAN Account and French Bank Account, to satisfy different cases.) . A bank account defines the Account/Routing Number, the bank address, etc of the Legal Entity.

 

o        US Bank Account

This is the for the bank account in US.

 

o        IBAN Account

This is the for international bank account.

 

o        French Bank Account

This is the for the bank account in France.

Folder Contacts

A contact is defined by its validation period, description and contact identity (including a name, a phone number, an address). The address of the contact is picked from the available addresses of the Legal Entity.

 

A contact is used for instance by the billing settings to define the recipient of the bill in a Group Benefit Plan.

Definition

A physical person is represented in Wynsure by an  instance of Person. As the same person may play several roles (Broker, Member, User), these roles do not inherit form person but are related to person.
A person can be in relation with another person. This relation can be mono directional (is Child of) or multi directional (are married).

Usage

·                                                                    Subscriber of Individual contracts

·                                                                    Linked to a Member of a Organization unit

·                                                                    Linked to a User of the application (See authorization Module)

·                                                                    A Broker can be a Person (See compensation module)

·                                                                    Contacts of a contract

·                                                                    Recipient of a Bill or Compensation Statement (See billing module)

·                                                                    Payer of a Payment (See accounting module)

·                                                                    Covered persons in a Contract subscribed coverage (See contract module). Person relations are used to allow candidates to be covered.

·                                                                    Etc.

 

Classification

Classifications applied to persons are defined globally in Wynsure.
When classifications are defined, and a user defines a member they have to specify in every classification which options applies.

 

Person classifications are used into Individual Product module to customize Rules (Pricing, subscription, etc.).

Summary

Model

Usage

A summary will be available when its involvement concerns a customer, no matter its a person or a legal entity.

 

It can be active either by double click on the customer line on the desktop or left click on the summary icon.

 

Main Screen

The Main Screen is composed of 2 parts, a part that shows the general information of this legal entity/person in the form of a file, another part that shows specific information about this legal entity/person, such as the contract subscribed, accounts, bills to pay, payment accomplished, disbursements, contacts, quotes, delivered service, members(this concerns only the legal entity) and claims. Generally, all operations concern the customer can be done here.

 

General Information summary

In this part, you can see the summary of some sub folders information defined in legal entity/person, in the following order: main folder, other correspondences, contacts, bank accounts and involvements. These information is performed in a blank window, you can also consult them in there original folder.

Specific Information summary

Sub Folder Contract

Contract includes here all kinds of defined insurance contracts, for individuals or for groups.

This folder shows all the contract subscribed. Moreover, you can subscribe a contract from here and only here.

Sub Folder Accounts

The account here is to record all the transfers concerning the current client. For example, you can define an invoice account for each contract and a suspense account for default transfers.

Sub Folder Bills

Bills will be generated since a contract is signed, according to the pricing rules, certain amount will be charged at certain time. 

In this folder, all the bills the customer should pay are listed, with information as bills status, invoice number, amount, period, concerned contract number, etc.

Sub Folder Payments

Once the customer accomplishes a payment, it will be recorded in this folder. Normally, each payment should correspond to a bill.

In this folder, all the payments of the customer are listed, detail information of the payment can be consulted too.

Sub Folder Disbursements

Disbursement will be achieved when a claim is announced and is approved.

Sub Folder Contacts

The contacts folder here records the content of each contact and follow up between the client and the Wynsure user, shown as  below.

Sub Folder Quotes

Wynsure allows to get a quote before the contract is signed. User can simulate the contract according to his preference and finally get a quote. He can either chose to valid this simulation or to dispose it. You can also get quote by the tree menu quote on left hand side of the desktop.

Sub Folder Delivered Services

This folder show the services that have been delivered to this customer.

Sub Folder Members

This folder list all employees in this legal entity. (This folder doesnt concern the person.)

Sub Folder Claims

Claim is the request for reimbursement of a loss covered by an insurance. Wynsure allows other person than the customer to declare for his claims. Claim declaration can only be done in this folder.

·                                                                    Enrolled in a Group Benefit Plan when Enrolment is detailed.

 

Main screen

The Screen contains,

o        Legal entity Name, which is referred to the legal entity the member belongs to and can display the corresponding legal entity main screen.

o        Person Name & Birth date, which is referred to the some one in Person and can display the persons main screen

Actor Desktop

Person Picker Desktop

From this desktop, you can search and show the person with certain criteria, for example the last name, the first name, the involvement, etc.  Birth date and Gender options are not considered as searching criteria till they are chosen.

 

You can also create new person through this desktop, and give information of the person, the detail information is presented in the chapter Person.

Legal Entity Picker Desktop

From this desktop, you can search and show the legal entity with certain criteria, for example the legal name, the short name, the involvement, classification, etc.   New legal entity can be created from this desktop, by giving all necessary information of this legal entity, as presented in the chapter Legal Entity.

 

Legal entity may contain some members, by clicking the edit member button when a legal entity is chosen, we can consult and edit its member information by the following window,

Customer Picker Desktop

All actors whose involvement contains Customer is consider as a customer, no matter it is a person or a legal entity. The creation of customer profile is not done here, instead, it should be done in the creation of a person/legal entity. You can only search and show customer on this desktop. However, there are different searching criteria for person and legal entity.

 

Customer as person is identified by his name and first name.

Customer as legal entity is identified by its legal name and short name.

 

Actors special processes

Termination

Management of the termination Date for History purposes and consistency checking of existing Contracts.

Propagation on termination on existing contracts of the concerned terminated entity. For instance, if the actor is a Legal Entity, the Legal Entity Termination process will manage its Group benefit plan termination.

 

Modification with As Of Date History management

Every actor is versioned in Wynsure.
Wynsure billing module takes into account actor versions, actor modifications to recalculate existing bills and manage adjustments Bill lines when the new calculated price is different from the already calculated amount. (See Billing module to have more detail)
