Definition

A compensation plan is the definition of what can be subscribed in a compensation contract, i.e. the coverage that can be compensated in the contract and the compensation rules associated to them.

A compensation plan is attached to an issuing company: when a compensation contract is subscribed on this plan, the contract is signed between the issuing company and the producer.

Usage

A compensation plan is necessary to provide the context of a contract. The compensation plan describes which coverage can be compensated in the contract; it also defines the rules to calculate the percentage applied on the premium.

Main screen of Life Insurance compensation plan

Main processes managed at the compensation plan level

 

·         One Compensation plan will be available for subscription between Effective Date and End Effective Date of the product.

·         When a compensation contract is terminated, it is possible to reinstate it if the reinstatement date is before the delay after termination defined in the product (see administration panel).

·         The compensation plan also defines the contract number generator that will be used to set the contract number of each compensation contract.

Offered compensation service

Model

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


Definition

 

The offered compensation service is the smallest element of a compensation plan that can be subscribed in a compensation contract.
When an offered compensation service is subscribed, a subscribed compensation service is created.

The offered compensation service defines the rule used to calculate the rate that will be applied to the premium.

Usage

The offered compensation service defines the rating rule and the coverage that can be compensated.
The rating rule is defined either as a flat rate, or as a scaled rate.

·         Flat rate: the flat rate defines a percentage that will be used during all the active period of the contract.

·         Scaled rate: the rate is defined by a table. The product designer can manipulate some business data to define the dimensions of the table. These business data can be:

o        The accumulated amount paid by the client owner of the compensation contract;

o        The accumulated year to date amount paid by the client;

o        The age of the compensated contract.

This list is easily customizable to use the chosen business data. For more details on Formula and Table, see Rule Engine Module description.
The compensation service will be available for subscription between the Effective Date and the End Effective Date.

Compensation contract

Definition

A compensation policy is an agreement between the producer and the insurance company defining the coverages that the producer can sell, and how he will be paid.

Usage

The compensation policy defines the contract and the associated coverages that will be compensated.
It also defines the billing properties (frequency, dates) associated to the compensation services (cf. Billing part).
The policy contains the effective dates (Begin date, Modification effective date) and information about termination if available.

Subscribed compensation service

Model

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


Definition

The subscribed compensation service defines the coverage for which the producer will be compensated and the associated rules.

Usage

A subscribed compensation service is used to provide the context of compensation. It is used to define which coverage will be compensated, and how it will be done.

Main processes managed at the subscribed compensation service level

The subscribed compensation service defines the split percentage: a producer can be compensated the total amount of a contract or only a portion of the contract defined by the split percentage.

The default behavior of the compensation service is to compensate on all of the billing services. The compensation can also be calculated on only one billing service.

The rule used to compute the compensation amount can be the rule defined in the compensation plan, a flat rate, a flat rate per enrolled member, or a flat percentage. This is defined in the subscribed compensation service.

Compensation Statement

Definition

 

The statement is the bill to be paid by the insurer to the producer.

Usage

The compensation statement is generated by the billing batch using the information from billing service (frequency, period length). The calculated premium depends on the subscribed and offered compensation services. It takes into account the premium paid for the compensated contract during the concerned period.
